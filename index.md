# Privacy Policy — AWS Exam Prep

**Last updated: June 2026**

## Overview

AWS Exam Prep (Cloud Exam Prep) is a mobile application for studying cloud certification exams (AWS, Azure, GCP, and others). This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

**All data is stored locally on your device only.** We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

The app stores the following data locally using Android SharedPreferences:

- Your quiz answers and accuracy scores (to track your progress)
- Spaced repetition scheduling data (to prioritize which questions to show you)
- Study streak information (days studied)
- Session history (scores from previous sessions)
- App preferences (dark mode setting)

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
- We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs
- We do not transmit any data to external servers
- We do not use advertising networks
- We do not share data with third parties

## Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your Android device in the app's private storage area. It is not accessible to other apps. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

## Children's Privacy

This app is intended for adults preparing for professional certifications. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
